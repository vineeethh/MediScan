About MediScan

AI-Powered Health Guidance for India

MediScan is an AI-powered health assistant built specifically for Indian users. It uses Claude AI (via OpenRouter) combined with a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline grounded in ICMR guidelines to provide reliable, contextually accurate health information.

We aim to reduce dependence on unreliable online sources and minimize health misinformation by providing evidence-based, accessible health insights tailored to the Indian context.
